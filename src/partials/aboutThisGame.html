About This Game

Color Fusion is developed by passionate team dedicated to creating innovative and visually appealing puzzle games. With its fluid animations, immersive sound effects, and easy-to-learn mechanics, Color Fusion offers an unforgettable gaming experience. Whether you're experimenting with colors, solving tricky puzzles, or simply unwinding with a visually captivating game, Color Fusion delivers endless hours of entertainment.

Color Fusion companion app allows you to explore the full range of capabilities of the Color Fusion Nail Polish Maker. Up your nail game with custom matching colors and unique nail art designs. Manicures have never been so fun!
